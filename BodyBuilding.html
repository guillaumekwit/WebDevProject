Bodybuilding

Advantages
  • Medium Rep Medium/low Weight
  • Pump focused lifts
  • Controlled lifts
  • Isolation lifts
  • Has a Healthy look on nutrition and diet
  • Physical health
    • one way to stay active and workout without having to do the BIG 3 (Squat, Bench, Dead)
  • Basic Strength
  • Supplementation
    • many bodybuilders will opt to make a protein shake post workouts
      as it is an easy fast and cheap way to get extra protein without having to cook anything.
Disadvantages
  • Character and Mentality
    • Most bodybuilders are extra critical about themselves and will most often get “body Dysmorphia”
  • Body Dysmorphia
    • this is a mental problem that is very common.
  • Little Freedom on diet
    • for those that are big into the bodybuilding lifestyle you have a very limited Diet.
      The stricter you are the cleaner you will eat.
  • Bodybuilding is a very small community, in 2022 it is growing and has gotten bigger.
    the downside is due to this you may feel like an outsider as most of the world does not understand what it takes
