BodyBuilding

  • Moderate amount of reps using moderate weight
  • Focused on the aesthetic of the physique
  • Emphasis on nutrition and dieting
  • The objective is to engage the most amount of target muscle as possible to induce muscular cell development, and growth

    • Calistenics

  • High amount of rep using body weight
  • Builds Functional Strength
  • Considered an inexpensive way to train
  • The objective is to improve mobility and strength in functional ways that help people stay healthy long term

    Powerlifting

  • Low amount of reps using high weight
  • Builds Raw Strength
  • Non-restrictive in diet
  • The objective is to imorove raw strength and power so that you can lift the most amount of weight physically possible
