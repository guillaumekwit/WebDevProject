Powerlifting

Advantages
  • Low rep high Weight
  • Primary focus is to build Strength
  • Good From and Full Range of Motion are important
  • P=D/T (power is equal to distance over time)
  • The objective is to move the most amount of weight safely in the shortest amount of time possible
  • Very clear Goal, Increase max Squat, Bench, Deadlifts.
Disadvantages
  • Focused too heavily on Maximal Strength
  • Good Form is Based on Lifting as Much as Possible
  • Too focused on the “Big-3”
    • Focus all there time on training only Squat, Bench and Deadlift
